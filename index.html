Drumming Background

Hello there! I've been playing the drums since I was 13 or so. I listened to a lot of grunge music at that age, and the drums seemed the most angsty. I was also rebelling against the piano lessons my mom made me take. Once my first drum teacher taught me how to hear the drums in any song, I learned music by ear.

I joined my first band when I was in college, back in 2005 and have been playing in various bands ever since. For me, the technical elements of drumming are important, but not as important as the heart and emotion a drummer brings to the instrument. I love drummers who play for the song and not just to hit the drums as loudly as they can and do flashy drum solos.
